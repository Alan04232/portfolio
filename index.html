Who I Am

I'm a B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Government Engineering College Idukki, affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU). Currently in my 6th semester, I focus on building real-world systems that matter.

My passion lies at the intersection of embedded systems and machine learning — designing IoT nodes that sense, communicate, and trigger intelligent decisions. I'm deeply interested in applying engineering to solve critical problems like disaster early warning and Space Exploration.

Beyond academics, I explore Linux systems, cybersecurity concepts, and robotics. I believe in building first, learning from iteration, and deploying systems that actually work in the field.
